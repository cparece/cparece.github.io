Latest News
  • Unique Coca-Cola Packaging at Galaxy's Edge

    There will be specialty themed Coca-Cola beverages throughout Walt Disney's Worlds new land Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge. The attention to detail within this immersive experience is unreal. Disney is really starting to take this to another level. Get ready for the opening of this experience in fall 2019.

  • Skyliner Update

    Disney's latest details are revealed on the Skyliner that will link several parks and resorts across Walt Disney World. The gondolas that will soar across the sky are now called "guest cabins". These cabins have a max capacity of 10 guests, with bench seating. Each gondola will be themed based on classic Disney favorites, as well as, Marvel Studio and LucasFilms.

  • Lion King Trailer

    The latest trailer for the Live Action version of the lion king has been released. THe iconic movie was originally created 25 years ago, this new live action take on the classic movie has people in awe. The amount of detail in the trailer is stunning. The movie will hit theaters on July 19, 2019.
