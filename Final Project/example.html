Visiting Maui

Hiking
Maui has so many wonderful places to hike and see the most breathtaking waterfalls. The Road to Hana is the most common place to go on the island to travel through the rainforest and find these hidden gems.
Snorkeling
Off the shores of Maui, you will find an outstanding abundance reefs. Within these reefs live so many different types of fish and coral. The best part about snorkeling in Hawaii is the ability to swim with Sea Turtles, almost anywhere you jump in the water you have a chance of seeing one of these majestic animals.
Flying
Soar through the sky in a helicopter where you can see Maui from above. The lush rainforest, waterfalls, beaches, craters and mountains are such a breathtaking view from above. To make your experience even more memorable, fly doors off. It will be the best part of your vacation!
